Who are you?

We are just one of the language learners of our world that got stuck with the lack of free tools to practice certain skills - in our case, conjugation and speaking practice ideas. In our unsuccessful search for the tools that would ease our language learning, we decided to create our own tools and offer it for the others. While we had lots of changes on the road, the goal of providing learning-helper-tools stayed the same and is reflected here on our website.

What do you offer?

Conjugation quiz for practicing your conjugation skills and speaking games to practice speaking given a topic.

Do you use AI?

We don't. Even though we have utilized AI tools while developing our website, no AI tool is integrated in our dynamic functionalities. This means that the practice material you see is available online and is not actively obtained from AI.
To be even more elaborate, the verb conjugation material is obtained from several dictionary websites using basic Python programming. On the other hand, for speaking games, once we have decided on the games, we have obtained the game cards from AI, since we did not have access to any free content, nor had the capacity to create our own.

Do you store any data?

Nothing other than what the internet stores without us choosing.
Verblingo is basically having a card game on your browser. As the children of 21st century, we are aware that there are hundreds of websites where you can get even more personalized exercises. However, by not requiring an account creation, we want to offer the freedom to practice languages, no strings attached.
At the end of the day, this is what we looked for when we searched for websites to practice, not another place to store our information, but a place to offer only help.
We are aware that personalized practice might be more beneficial and sought after. We are considering also offering that option in the future, but currently, we only offer non-personalized practice.

What languages do you have?

For now, we only offer Standard German language.

How do I use it?

No account required. Just click on the type of practice you want and start practicing. Find out about it on the main page!

Do you plan to offer other languages or other practice options?

We are considering upscaling our services based on demand, but currently, we are busy maintaining the existing features.
