Vocabulary Chain

Vocabulary Chain is a verbal game for two or more players. The players sit in a circle. Given 4 words, the first player forms one sentence that includes at least 2 of the words. The next player continues. The sentences do not need to be connected. Click the used words to receive new words for the next player.


Story Chain

Story Chain is a verbal game for two or more players. The players sit in a circle. Given 2 words, the first player starts a story using or related to at least one of the words. The next player continues. Click the used words to receive new words for the next player.


What if...?

What if...? is a verbal game for two or more players. The players sit in a circle. Given a what if question, each player responds in order of their choosing. When everyone has replied, click the question to receive another question.


Roleplay

Roleplay is a verbal game for two or more players. Given a situation and roles involved in the situation, the players choose a role or invent a new role and act out the situation. Click the situation to receive another situation.

Variation: Guess the Situation

Guess the Situation is a verbal game for two or more players. The players are divided into two groups. Given a situation and roles involved in the situation, one group plays the roleplay game, while the other group tries to guess what the situation is. Click the situation to receive another situation.


20 Questions

20 Questions is a verbal game for two or more players. One player is given an item. The other players try to guess what that item is by asking 20 yes/no questions. The round ends when the item is guessed or 20 questions have been asked. The person who guesses the item correctly, plays next. Click the item to receive a new item for the next player.
